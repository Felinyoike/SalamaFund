POST

/api/v1/score-and-disburse

Calculates the Climate Credit Score from satellite climate data and cooperative revenue, then returns a risk-tiered disbursement decision.

Request Parameters

farmer_id · stringrequired
latitude · floatrequired
longitude · floatrequired
requested_loan_amount · floatrequired
monthly_coop_revenue_ksh · floatrequired
requested_asset_id · stringoptional

Scoring Model

Climate Credit Score (300–850) = mapped sum of three layers:

Environmental Resilience (CHIRPS SPI + MODIS NDVI)0–300
Financial Capacity (revenue + velocity)0–350
Asset Mitigation Buffer0–150

Dynamic Disbursement Tiers

The limit scales continuously with the score inside each band — it is not a flat per-tier amount.

700–850 · Low RiskKSh 75,000 – 150,000
550–699 · ModerateKSh 25,000 – 75,000
300–549 · High RiskKSh 10,000 – 25,000

Example Request

curl -X POST http://localhost:5000/api/v1/score-and-disburse \ -H "Content-Type: application/json" \ -d '{ "farmer_id": "FARM-NRK-0192", "latitude": -0.3031, "longitude": 36.0662, "monthly_coop_revenue_ksh": 45000, "requested_loan_amount": 90000, "requested_asset_id": "ASSET-SOLAR-PUMP-04" }'
